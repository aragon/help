Troubleshooting

You can find in the following section a list of common problems and the most likely solutions to solve them. Support is also available on Discord, Spectrum, and forum.aragon.org.

1. I can't see my ANJ in the Dashboard.

Please try the following steps:

  1. Make sure that your account is connected to the Dashboard.
  2. If you own multiple accounts, make sure that the right account is selected.
  3. Make sure that you are connected to the Ethereum mainnet network.
  4. Refresh your browser's page.

2. I activated ANJ but I can't see my probability of being drafted.

When ANJ are activated, you must wait until the current term is over for them to increase your probability of being drafted. You can see the current term and its remaining duration at the top of the Dashboard.

3. When I tried to convert ANT to ANJ using convert.aragon.org the third transaction failed

There are three transactions necessary to complete an ANT-to-ANJ conversion on convert.aragon.org:

  1. Approve
  2. Open buy order
  3. Claim buy order

Sometimes a connection issue can cause the third transaction to fail. Follow these instructions to claim your order.

  1. Go to the Write Contract page for the ANJ bonding curve contract on Etherscan
  2. Click "Connect to Web3" at the top of the page and approve the connection in your Ethereum provider
  3. Scroll down to 10. claimBuyOrder
  4. In the _buyer (address) field paste the Ethereum address you placed your order with
  5. In the _batchId (uint256) field enter the block number of the block that your openBuyOrder transaction was confirmed in; this is the second transaction you sent when making the conversion
  6. In the _collateral (address) field enter the ANT contract address 0x960b236a07cf122663c4303350609a66a7b288c0
  7. Click "write", make sure the gas limit entered in your Ethereum provider is 500,000 or more, then sign and send the transaction. Once the transaction is confirmed, you should receive your ANJ in your address.

4. When I tried to convert ANJ to ANT using convert.aragon.org the second transaction failed

There are two transactions necessary to complete an ANJ-to-ANT conversion on convert.aragon.org:

  1. Open sell order
  2. Claim sell order

Sometimes a connection issue can cause the second transaction to fail. Follow these instructions to claim your order.

  5. In the _batchId (uint256) field enter the block number of the block that your openSellOrder transaction was confirmed in; this is the second transaction you sent when making the conversion
